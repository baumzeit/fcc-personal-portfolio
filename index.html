Space Odyssey  |  A Journey of Homecoming

Educational tabletop RPG for players of all ages.Easily accessible and carried by strong narration and vivid imagery the game implicitly asks questions about life, responsibility and friendship.

It is designed to help intuitively understand the most intricate issues that humanity is dealing with right now in a creative and joyful way.

The game is set in a colorful science-fiction universe with super cool space adventure characters who are banding up together to travel to amazing and strange planets while exploring deeper what it means to be human..

Web of Life  |  A Platform Based on Organic UI

An open and participative web platform living and growing by collective intelligence.Liberating the linear and analytical structure of "Search & Find" by keyword the design of the platform opens up towards relational ways of navigation and information gathering.

An innovative visualization system helps put cold data into context and thereby genarating 'warm data'. Data that includes transcontextual information, making apparent the necessary relations of otherwise conventionally isolated spheres of human experience.

Disentangle  |  A Framework for Collaborating Wisely

A framework designed for teams and individuals to approach issues in an encompassing and integrative way.The actual physical package consists of the 'orientation-book' (setting the context), the 'note-book' (pre-equipped with the introductory set of cards), a block of empty cards and my favourite pen.

Enthusiasm and passion - however well-intentioned - often seduces us to act hastily. But it is precisly there, where the kinds of epistemological errors and entanglements are planted that with time grow to undermine the very effect we hoped to achieve.
This framework introduces a epistemological shift towards organizing around our goals wisely.
